GDPR Compliance

Last Updated: March 2026

FiretechERP is committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This page explains how we handle personal data for users in the European Economic Area (EEA).

Your Rights Under GDPR

📋

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time.

✏️

Right to Rectification

You can update or correct inaccurate personal data directly from your account settings.

🗑️

Right to Erasure

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data.

⏸️

Right to Restrict Processing

You can ask us to limit how we use your personal data in certain circumstances.

📦

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) to transfer to another service.

🚫

Right to Object

You can object to data processing for direct marketing or profiling purposes at any time.

Data Controller

FiretechERP acts as the Data Controller for personal data collected through our SaaS platform. Our customers act as Data Processors for any personal data they store within the system.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to deliver our services
  • Legitimate interests — fraud prevention, security, system improvements
  • Consent — for marketing communications (always opt-in)

Contact Our DPO

To exercise your rights or raise a GDPR concern, contact us at mytechfms@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.
