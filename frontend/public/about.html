About FiretechERP

"We built FiretechERP because we couldn't find a field service ERP that was actually great to use. So we built one ourselves."

Our Story

FiretechERP was founded by a team with deep roots in field service operations. We watched companies manage critical operations through messy spreadsheets, disconnected tools, and outdated desktop software — and we knew there had to be a better way.

We set out to build a modern, cloud-native ERP that was powerful enough for enterprise teams but approachable enough for small companies growing fast. The result is FiretechERP — an all-in-one platform that connects customers, work orders, inventory, invoicing, and your entire field workforce.

Our Mission

To give field service companies the operational clarity and tools they need to deliver exceptional service, scale confidently, and get paid faster — all from one unified platform.
